## What Improved

- The main brand signal now matches the existing identity: red Chinese seal, warm paper, ink navy, and restrained jade.
- Primary calls to action and active navigation use Hồng/seal red instead of teal.
- The mobile flow keeps stroke practice hidden until the learner chooses to reveal or review it, which avoids giving away the answer during recall.
- The mock exam screen is visually stricter and keeps red as a disciplined brand/action color, not just an error color.

## Implementation Notes

- Map primary buttons, active tabs, and selected navigation to `--brand`/`--brand-strong`.
- Keep jade for progress, success, calm secondary controls, and small chart accents.
- Keep the app background closer to warm paper than pure white.
- Use red carefully for both brand and errors: errors should add icon/text/border semantics so they are not distinguished by color alone.
